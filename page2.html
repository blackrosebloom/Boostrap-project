Crew & Cast Jobs

When you in crew you will pick what kind of jobs you want for helping out. there is props,painting, building, lights, costume, & business.

For building you build the scent or extra parts in the scene & painting you just paint the scent that the building crew made or add color.

For props you find the item, costume they made them or use old clothing in the closet, business is just talking about when the show or what else they need, & light control to shut them down or where the person going.
